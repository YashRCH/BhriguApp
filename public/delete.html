Data Deletion Request

Bhrigu AI respects your privacy and your right to be forgotten. If you wish to completely and permanently delete your account and all associated personal data from our servers, you have two options:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Instant & Automated)

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the Bhrigu AI app:

  1. Open the Bhrigu AI app and ensure you are logged in.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the very bottom and tap the red "Delete Account" button.
  4. Confirm the prompt. Your account, profile, chats, charts, and social connections will be instantly and irrevocably purged from our databases.

Option 2: Email Request (Manual Process)

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot log in, you can submit a manual data deletion request to our Data Protection Officer.

Please note that for security purposes, you must send this email from the exact email address associated with your Bhrigu AI account so we can verify your identity.
