Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

Welcome to Bhrigu AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Bhrigu AI mobile application (the "App"), including astrology readings, chat, notifications, and Circle social features.

Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your Bhrigu AI account and associated app data at any time.

  • Inside the App: Open Bhrigu AI, go to your Profile screen, choose Delete Account, type confirm, and submit the deletion request.
  • If you cannot access the App: Email support.bhriguai@gmail.com from the email address connected to your Bhrigu AI account and write "Delete my Bhrigu AI account" in the subject.
  • Deleted data: We delete your user profile, authentication account, astrological profile history, chat history, readings, rewards, public Circle profile, username reservation, invite records, Circle connections, and reports submitted by you.
  • Deleting some data without deleting your account: You can delete saved Tarot, Geomancy, and Bhrigu Match readings from their history screens, and you can remove or block Circle connections from the Circle connection screen.
  • Data we may keep: We may retain limited safety, abuse-prevention, payment, legal, or dispute records only when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or enforcement of our user safety rules.
  • Timing: Account deletion requests are processed as soon as technically possible after verification. Backup, security, or legal-retention copies may take longer to expire from our systems.

1. Information We Collect

To provide personalized astrology insights, account features, and Circle connections, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you log in using Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to securely authenticate your profile.
  • Astrological Profile Data: To calculate your Vedic and Western birth charts accurately, we collect your date of birth, exact time of birth, and place of birth.
  • Chat Conversations: Messages exchanged with our AI Astrology Guru are processed to generate real-time Vedic astrology answers.
  • Circle Social Data: If you use Circle, we collect your username, public display profile, chart summary, invite codes, connection requests, relationship type selected by you, shared Circle compatibility readings, and block/remove status.
  • User Reports and Safety Signals: If you report Circle activity or AI content, we collect your selected reason, optional comment, app version, platform, and a short preview of the reported content so we can review safety issues.
  • Notification Tokens: If you allow notifications, we store Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens to deliver app notifications.
  • Technical and Usage Data: We collect crash logs, performance diagnostics, and anonymous usage statistics via Firebase to monitor and improve application stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To compute and render your custom Western and Vedic birth charts (Cosmic Blueprints).
  • To deliver daily personalized horoscopes and interactive AI astrology readings.
  • To let you search usernames, create invite links, send or accept Circle connection requests, and generate shared compatibility or daily energy readings.
  • To provide safety tools such as Circle guideline acceptance, user reporting, remove, and block controls.
  • To send notifications when you have granted permission.
  • To maintain and secure your account access via Google Sign-In.
  • To identify bugs, analyze crash trends, and optimize overall app performance.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data extremely seriously:

  • All personal, profile, and authentication data is stored securely on Google Firebase Cloud infrastructure.
  • All communications between the App and our backend services are encrypted in transit using standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • We retain your birth data only for as long as your account exists to continuously serve your astrological calculations.
  • Circle data is retained while your account exists or while a connection remains active, unless removed, blocked, or deleted as part of account deletion.
  • Safety reports may be retained where necessary to investigate abuse, enforce our user safety rules, prevent repeat misuse, or meet legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Disclosures

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers. Data is disclosed only in the following limited cases:

  • Google Firebase: For user authentication, cloud database storage, and secure hosting.
  • AI Processing APIs: Anonymized birth chart coordinates may be sent to secure AI models to formulate your cosmic answers.
  • Circle Connections: People you connect with in Circle can see your public display profile, username, chart summary, selected relationship type, and shared Circle readings for that connection.
  • User-Initiated Sharing: If you share an invite link or code through another app, that sharing is controlled by you and the destination app.
  • Legal and Safety: We may disclose limited information if required by law or needed to protect users, investigate abuse, or enforce our policies.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You retain full ownership and control over your personal data:

  • You may access or update your astrological profile details inside the App settings at any time.
  • You may remove or block a Circle connection from inside the Circle connection screen.
  • You may report Circle activity or AI content from the in-app report controls.
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated app data, including your user profile, astrological profile history, public Circle profile, username reservation, invite records, Circle connections, and reports submitted by you. Some safety records may be retained only where required for abuse prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling.
